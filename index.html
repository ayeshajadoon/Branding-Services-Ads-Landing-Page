WHY BRAND
GUIDES MATTER

To stand out from your competitors, you must have a strong brand. Going through the process of creating a brand guide allows you to develop your brand positioning.

The guide then creates a way to consistently tell that story. For the consumer, consistency builds trust. And trust builds growth. An inconsistent brand makes people hesitate. A clear, confident brand makes them say "yes" faster.

Plus, your team will love having one source of truth instead of Slack messages like, "Wait, is it the pink logo or the dark red one?"
